OUTGUN

Let's play

Descriptions

Rather than a person using a gun to protect someone, in Outgun, you play as the gun itself to protect a person. Play as a pistol, sub machine gun or rocket launcher and bounce around in the air from the recoil when you fire.

Game mechanics

Going to the person you are protecting will reload your gun. The pistol, sub machine gun, and rocket launcher shoot differently:

  • Pistol: single fire, 25 bullets
  • Sub machine gun: full auto, 30 bullets
  • Rocket launcher: single fire, has an explosive area of effect, 7 bullets

Enemies have a 15% chance to drop a sub machine gun or rocket launcher that you collide into to switch weapons. Shooting will bounce the gun away from the crosshair from the recoil.
